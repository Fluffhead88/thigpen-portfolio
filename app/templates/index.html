About Me

I am a Web-based Software Developer and Product Manager who specializes in leading teams to provide timely and complete solutions tailored to specific business needs and goals. After spending nearly a decade working in business management and running a multimillion dollar retail store, I know what truly drives successful businesses, and how to find real world solutions in today’s work environments. Since then, I've spent several years learning the latest technologies employed in web and software development with a strong full stack skillset as well as leading development teams and interacting with customers to find solutions that work for them with extremely fast turnaround times.

I'm also a professional musician who has been performing and working around the Southeast for 20+ years. My experience as a creative artist brings a unique eye and approach to composing and arranging solutions as well as the ability to pivot and be flexible when change is present.

My 2 current musical projects include The Grateful Brothers, a tribute to The Allman Brothers Band and The Grateful Dead and Cash Machine an original funk-rock band. I handle management, merch, musical direction, booking, social media, and maintain the website for The Grateful Brothers.

I hold a Bachelor of Arts in audio technology and a minor in business administration from Clemson University and a Carolina Code School Immersive 12 Week Full Stack Certificate.

I am currently a Product Mangager at Medprocure LLC, leading the SmartPO team, and a lead software developer at Quattlebaum Servcies Corp and regularly work in PHP, Laravel, React, Python, Django, HTML, CSS, Azure DevOps, Product Management, Native Mobile Apps, Modern Reposonsive Web Design, Apache web server, Linux sys-admin, MySql database implementation and management, GitHub, ClickUp, GitLab, Heroku, API development and integrations, Project Planng & Management, long-term business success planning, interviewing, hiring, and much more.
